About Our Organization

Self-organized Biological and Medical Engineering students

We strive to be the best and
make awesome work.

Our mission is to promote interdisciplinary work by generating a student community, creating instances of networking with students and professors from different faculties and from external institutions such as colleges and other universities.

Our vision is to to be a world-class interdisciplinary student organization, both in teaching and research.

Our main objectives are to do interdisciplinary projects with impact in society and to reduce knowledge barriers between different areas, by creating educational resources.
